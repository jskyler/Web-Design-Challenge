Max Temperature




The graph above comparing Latitude vs Humidity data was captured (11/14/2020). The data appears to indicate the Max Temperatures were higher near the equator and in the southern hemisphere. Lower Max Temperatures were found in the northern hemisphere and continue to decrease as the distance from the equator increases. This is expected during that time of year due to the earth's rotation.
